Delicious No Bake Cookies

These easy no-bake cookies combine cocoa, oats, and peanut butter into a rich, chewy treat that comes together in minutesâ€”no oven required, perfect for busy days or beginner bakers.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, cocoa powder (Optional), and salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently.
  3. Once boiling, let it cook for 1 minute without stirring.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla extract until smooth.
  5. Add the oats and mix until fully combined.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto wax paper and let them cool until set.
  7. Enjoy your delicious no-bake cookies!
